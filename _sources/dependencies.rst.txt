************
Dependencies
************

ODToolkit is built on top of several Python packages. The packages used to develop this toolkit are listed below.

+--------------+----------------------------+----------------+------------------+
| Package Name | Usage in ODToolkit         | Latest Version | Required Version |
+==============+============================+================+==================+
| Matplotlib_  | Plotting evaluation score  | |matplotlib|   | 3.0.2            |
+--------------+----------------------------+----------------+------------------+
| Pandas_      | Data preprocessing         | |pandas|       | 0.23.4           |
+--------------+----------------------------+----------------+------------------+
| Numpy_       | Main data structure        | |numpy|        | 1.16.0           |
+--------------+----------------------------+----------------+------------------+
| Scipy_       | Built-in models            | |scipy|        | 1.0.0            |
+--------------+----------------------------+----------------+------------------+
| Keras_       | Built-in models            | |keras|        | 2.2.4            |
+--------------+----------------------------+----------------+------------------+
| Levenshtein_ | Name ontology              | |levenshtein|  | 0.12.0           |
+--------------+----------------------------+----------------+------------------+
| Dateutil_    | Formatting raw data        | |dateutil|     | 2.8.0            |
+--------------+----------------------------+----------------+------------------+
| Seaborn_     | Plotting estimation result | |seaborn|      | 0.9.0            |
+--------------+----------------------------+----------------+------------------+
| Scikit_      | Built-in models            | |scikit|       | 0.20.3           |
+--------------+----------------------------+----------------+------------------+
