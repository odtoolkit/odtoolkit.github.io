How to get involved?
====================

We welcome various types of contributions such as bug reports, pull requests, etc., and encourage the community to
improve and augment this toolkit by adding their occupancy estimation models and/or their data sets.
Feel free to reach us through `Github <https://github.com/sustainable-computing/ODToolkit>`_ with regard to any
concerns you might have.

If you find this toolkit helpful, we appreciate if you cite our publication.
